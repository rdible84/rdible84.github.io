Private Equity / Software|Chief Executive Officer

CEO succession for an investor-backed software platform

The challenge

A PE-backed platform approaching its next growth phase needed to transition from a founder-CEO without unsettling the management team, customers, or the value-creation plan.

Our approach

We helped the board define the future-state role against the investment thesis, benchmarked two internal contenders, and ran a discreet external market map to pressure-test the slate.

The outcome

The board appointed an external CEO with a calibrated 100-day plan. The internal contenders were retained in expanded roles, and the transition closed without business disruption.

Financial Services / Regulated|Independent Director

Independent director search for a regulated financial services firm

The challenge

Heightened regulatory scrutiny meant the board needed an independent director with audit-committee depth and credibility with the regulator, on a compressed timeline.

Our approach

We mapped board capability gaps against the governance agenda, defined the required regulatory and sector profile, and delivered a tightly calibrated slate for committee and investor review.

The outcome

The firm appointed a director who chaired the audit committee within two quarters and strengthened the board's standing in its next regulatory review.

Growth-Stage Technology|Chief Revenue Officer

Commercial leadership hire during a growth reset

The challenge

A founder-led business missing plan needed a revenue leader who could install operating rigor while working inside a fast-changing, founder-centric executive team.

Our approach

We reframed the brief around operating range and culture-add rather than logo pedigree, assessed finalists against the specific change environment, and supported the offer and onboarding.

The outcome

The placed CRO rebuilt the commercial operating model and returned the business to its growth trajectory within three quarters.
